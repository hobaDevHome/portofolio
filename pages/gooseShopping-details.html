Goose Shopping : E-commerce React APP

The React E-Commerce App is a dynamic online shopping platform designed to offer a seamless shopping experience for users. This application features a product listing page with detailed product descriptions, product filtering options by category and color, and a secure payment processing system using Stripe. Users can not only shop for their favorite items but also keep track of their purchase history.
